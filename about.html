About EC Finder

Empowering Students to Discover Their Path

EC Finder was created to help high school students navigate the overwhelming world of extracurricular opportunities and find activities that truly match their interests and goals.

Our Mission

We believe every student deserves access to opportunities that can help them grow, learn, and pursue their passions. EC Finder simplifies the search process by organizing hundreds of competitions, programs, internships, and awards into one easy-to-use platform.

Whether you're interested in STEM, arts, humanities, or community service, EC Finder helps you discover opportunities that align with your grade level, interests, and schedule.

Dylan Chandran

Creator & Developer

Dylan Chandran is a sophomore at Monte Vista High School with a passion for computer science and linguistics. Recognizing the challenge students face in finding meaningful extracurricular activities, Dylan created EC Finder to make the discovery process easier and more accessible for everyone.
